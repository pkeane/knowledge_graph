## Your values, in brief

From `/Users/pkeane/VALUES.md` (updated April 2026):

## In the graph — your likely high-value reading list

### On inequality as threat to democracy (new emphasis)

- [[reich-robert]] — the vicious cycle of economic and political power, most clearly argued; *Saving Capitalism*, *The System*.
- [[hacker-jacob]], [[pierson-paul]] — *Winner-Take-All Politics*, *American Amnesia*, *Let Them Eat Tweets*: inequality as politically produced, not market-natural.
- [[piketty-thomas]] — the empirical spine (*r > g*).
- [[stiglitz-joseph]] — the information-economics version of the same argument.
- [[giridharadas-anand]] — the critique of philanthropic laundering of the arrangement.
- [[putnam-robert]] — the civic preconditions the arrangement corrodes.

### On digital public services / platform power (new emphasis)

- [[zuboff-shoshana]] — *The Age of Surveillance Capitalism*; the fullest theoretical account of the non-partisan digital gap.
- [[wu-tim]] — net neutrality, *The Curse of Bigness*, the New Brandeisian antitrust argument.
- [[doctorow-cory]] — "enshittification," interoperability, the right of exit.
- [[mazzucato-mariana]] — the public origins of the technologies the platforms enclosed.

### On regulated capitalism / strong public sector

- [[polanyi-karl]] — "fictitious commodities" and the "double movement"; the cleanest theoretical underwriting of "capitalism but regulated."
- [[keynes-jm]], [[welfare-state]] — the mid-century settlement.
- [[galbraith-john-kenneth]] — *The Affluent Society*, countervailing power, "private affluence and public squalor."
- [[minsky-hyman]] — financial instability hypothesis.
- [[robinson-joan]] — Cambridge Keynesian.
- [[krugman-paul]] — the contemporary Keynesian public voice.
- [[mazzucato-mariana]] — the entrepreneurial state; mission-oriented industrial policy.
- [[jacobs-jane]] — cities, public space, and the democratic conditions of community.
- [[harvey-david]], [[klein-naomi]] — the contemporary case against unregulated capital.
- [[anderson-elizabeth]] — *Private Government*: the workplace as unaccountable authoritarian state.
